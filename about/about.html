Description

Tajikistan is a landlocked country located in Central Asia, bordered by Afghanistan to the south, Uzbekistan to the west, Kyrgyzstan to the north, and China to the east. With a population of around 9 million people, Tajikistan is predominantly mountainous and has a diverse ethnic makeup, with Tajiks making up the majority of the population. The country's official language is Tajik, which is closely related to Persian, and its capital city is Dushanbe. Tajikistan has a rich cultural heritage, with influences from ancient Persia, Islamic civilization, and Soviet-era modernization.
